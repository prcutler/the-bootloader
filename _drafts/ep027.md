### Covert Regional Communication with Meshtastic (Paul #1)
If you've ever wanted to learn more about [Meshtastic](https://meshtastic.org) and how it works, this is the talk for you.
Daryll Strauss gave a talk titled [Covert Regional Communication with Meshtastic](https://www.youtube.com/live/2_xXU-u4QTQ?si=NUwnZBTXSOZK1ori), which was the last talk of the day at Supercon.

Daryll covers what Meshtastic is, his motivations for using it, and how to make a covert communication system using Meshtastic. His goal in making it covert was to support his local communities during these troubling times, as well as:
* No eavesdropping
* Limited geographic area
* Difficult to locate transmissions
* Psuedononymous
