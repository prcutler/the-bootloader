### Statimatic STM-01 Automatic Turntable (Paul #1)

Patrick Nelson started this project about 4 years ago and then got stuck - but now he’s back and he’s documenting all of his progress to build an open source turntable.

His project goals included:

* Only 7” records at 33 1/3 rpm or 45 rpm
* Full sized LP expansion
* Expansion port
  * Record changer
  * Alarm clock
  * Remote control from a Commodore 64
* Automatic capabilities - the tonearm plays and stops on its own
* Easily sourced or made parts
  * Open source design and PCB files
* Anti-skate, speed control, track lightly
  * Treat records nicely

From there, Patrick shares additional videos on the design, how he redesigned the tonearm and a another video for its lift mechanism, and more.

### 3D Printing News Roundup (Paul #2)
* [Tool Trace AI](https://www.tooltrace.ai/) is a site that allows you to upload a picture of your tools and it will create foam shadowboxes or grifinity inserts you can 3D print to hold your tools.
* [3D printing uses AI to fix prints in real time](https://www.tomshardware.com/3d-printing/researchers-use-agentic-ai-to-monitor-and-correct-3d-prints-system-catches-errors-in-real-time-uses-modular-design-to-work-on-different-makes-and-models) - using multiple LLMs, researchers have created a way for AI to monitor, correct, and fix your 3D prints in real time.

### Game Controllers (Paul #3)
* The [OpenSplitDeck](https://github.com/tommybee456/OpenSplitDeck) is a home built gamepad controller inspired by Valve's Steam Controller. It features two trackpads, joysticks, and more and can also split into two halves to make it more ergonomic. [Linus Tech Tips YouTube video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=eNb55ZwnCRc)

* [Joypad OS](https://github.com/joypad-ai/joypad-os) ([Adafruit coverage](https://blog.adafruit.com/2026/01/23/joypad-os-universal-game-controller-firmware/)) allows you to build a dongle to connect your game controller to other platforms.  The dongle is powered by the rp2040 and you can buy a dev kit for $50 at [Joypad.AI](https://joypad.ai).
